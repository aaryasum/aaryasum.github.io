**Spring 2025** — Course Assistant for **ME314: Robotic Dexterity**, taught by Prof. Monroe Kennedy.

ME314 focuses on the principles of dexterous robotic manipulation, including kinematics, motion planning, manipulator and gripper control, contact modeling, and grasp stability. The course integrates perception and learning-based methods, and emphasizes hands-on projects with real robotic manipulators performing contact-rich tasks.

Course website: https://arm.stanford.edu/courses/robotic-dexterity/
